Overview

Apply for firearm licence

  1. 1. Check you're eligible for licence

    Before applying, check you eligible for a firearm licence so to avoid wasting time:

    • Check eligibility

  2. 2. Give contact details

    • Give name and address
    • Give date of birth

  3. 3. Nominate two referees

    To apply you need two people who can act as referees and vouch for you:

    Check a person is eligible to be a reference
    • Give contact details for referees

  4. 4. Prove identity

    To apply you need to prove your age and identity:

    • Prove identity with GOV.UK Verify

  5. 5. Give permission to check medical details

    To ensure firearms are used safely, medical information is needed for each licence holder:

    • Answer questions about your medical history
    • Give GP contact details

  6. 6. Upload photograph

    To use a firearm licence, you will need to prove your identity in person, which a photograph makes possible:

    • Take photograph
    • Upload photograph

  7. 7. List firearms you want to own

    Give calibre, type, make, reason for each firearm you want to own:

    • List fireams

  8. 8. Describe place firearm would be stored

    You must give your chosen supplier a contract before they start work.

    • Read guidance on secure keeping of firearms.
    • Answer questions about firearm storage
